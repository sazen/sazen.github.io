Thyroglobulin:
Thyroglobulin (thyrobolulin, TG) is synthesized by the epithelial cells of the thyroid follicular a glycoprotein molecules , is the main component of the thyroid follicular colloid , globulin synthesized in the form of thyroid hormone is stored in the follicular lumen. Under normal circumstances, only a trace amount of TG into the blood circulation.
